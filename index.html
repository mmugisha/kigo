OUR HOTEL

Each room in our boutique hotel has beautiful views of lake victoria. In the evening, you can enjoy gorgeous sunsets and take in the cool lake breeze as you sip your drink on the large balconies attached to every room. our friendly staff will prepare for you delicious breakfast with all-natural ingredients every morning during your stay.

Touring In Uganda

Our experienced team can tailor-made a tour of Uganda's beautiful scenery and wildlife just for you. We organize tours to see the mountain gorillas at Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.You can also see the tree-climbing lions, giraffes, rhinos, cheetahs and other animals at the expansive Queen Elizabeth National Park.You can also see the powerful Nile River or visit the thunderous Murchison's Fall in the North-West Uganda.

ROOMS

NEIGHBOURHOOD

The Estate is located about ten minutes from Lake Victoria Serena Hotel which has a beautiful 18-hole golf course along the Lake Victoria shores. The city of kampala is a 15-30 minutes drive depending on traffic.

TOURING SITES IN UGANDA

Dubbed the "pearl of Africa", the small but incredibly diverse nation of Uganda remains a fascinating yet vastly underexplored destination. It boasts some beautiful national parks that are home to a variety of plants, birds and animal species.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Its located in Western Uganda and its famous for its exotic wildlife, the home to many elephants, hippos, Congo lions and many others.

Bwindi Impenetrable

It lies in Southwestern Uganda on the edge of the Rift Valley.It provides shelter to further 120 mammals including baboons and chimpanzees, as well as elephants and antelopes.

Kidepo Valley National Park

It lies in the rugged semi-arid valleys between Uganda's boarders with Sudan and Kenya,some 700km from Kampala.It hosts over 77 mammals species as well as around 475 bird species.
